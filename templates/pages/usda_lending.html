USDA Loans

USDA loans are mortgages backed by the U.S. Department of Agriculture as part of its ‘Rural Development Guaranteed Housing Loan’ program.

They are available to home buyers with low-to-average income for their area. Offering up to 100% financing with reduced mortgage insurance premiums and below-market mortgage rates.

USDA Loan Requiments:

  • Based on the buyer and the property
  • Be in a qualified rural area - defined by USDA as having a population under 20,000
  • Meet USDA monthly income caps
  • Meet ability to replay standards (income eligibility, credit score, existing debt ratio
